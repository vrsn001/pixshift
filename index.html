How it works

01

Drop your files

Drag & drop or click to upload images and videos in any format — JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV, GIF, TIFF, AVIF, BMP, SVG, and more.

02

Pick your settings

Choose from 10 output resolutions (original up to 4K) and dial in your quality level. Aspect ratios are always preserved.

03

Convert & download

Click convert. Files are processed directly in your browser using the Canvas API. Download individually or grab all as a ZIP.

Zero data leaves your device

Conversion happens entirely in your browser via the Canvas API. No servers. No uploads. No logs. Complete privacy.

Instant processing

No waiting for uploads or server queues. Conversion runs in real-time using your device's GPU acceleration through the Canvas API.

Batch conversion

Upload and convert dozens of files at once. Each file is processed independently with its own progress indicator.

Lossless quality option

Set quality to 100 for near-lossless output. WebP achieves up to 30% smaller files than JPEG at equivalent visual quality.

Video frame extraction

Upload MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM, or MKV files. Pixshift extracts a high-quality frame and converts it to a crisp WebP still.

10 resolution presets

From 320×240 all the way to 4K UHD. Or keep the original resolution with perfect aspect ratio preservation at every size.

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression. WebP images are typically 25–34% smaller than comparable JPEGs and 26% smaller than PNGs.
